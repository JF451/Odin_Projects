Chicken Parmesan

Description

This is a recipe for Chicken Parmesan

Ingrediants

  • Chicken
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Eggs
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Flour
  • Olive Oil
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mozzarella
  • Basil
  • Provolone Cheese

Steps

  1. Pound Chicken to an even thickness
  2. Add Salt and Pepper
  3. Add Parmesan to the bread crumbs
  4. Rest before cooking
  5. Easy on sauce
  6. Heat Oven and put in
